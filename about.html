About Libflix

Empowering Library Science aspirants with quality education and resources since 2020

Home / About Us

Your Trusted Partner in LIS Education

Libflix was born from a simple observation: Library Science aspirants across India struggled to find quality study materials, solved papers, and expert guidance in one place. What started as a small YouTube channel sharing solved papers has now grown into a comprehensive learning platform serving thousands of students.

We believe that quality education should be accessible to everyone. That's why all our core resources remain completely free, while we continue to expand our library of materials to cover every aspect of UGC NET, SET, and other library science examinations.

Today, Libflix stands as a testament to what passionate educators and a supportive community can achieve together. Our resources have helped hundreds of aspirants clear their exams and achieve their dreams of becoming librarians and information professionals.
